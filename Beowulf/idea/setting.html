Setting

The story set in Scandinavia, nearly 500 AD. There were two tribal groups, which is the Geats and the Scyldings. Every kingdom has a mead-hall located at the center place. People told stories, celebrating in the mead-hall. However, outside the hall is endless danger and darkness.

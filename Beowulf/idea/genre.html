Genre

Epic heroic writing

A long poem telling the story of a legendary warrior. Beowulf can be seen as an elegy because it mourns the loss of a set of heroic values that may have been tragically misguided in the first place. In heroic epics, the warrior hero sets a moral example: through his stories, the value and meaning of a society's moral code can be tested.

First part

Beowulf left Geatland with his king’s permission to help Hrothgar for killing Grendel. Beowulf fought with Grendel without any weapons because Beowulf thought it is unfair if he won Grendel. After a fierce fight. Finally, Beowulf tore Grendel’s arm from his body. Grendel ran to the marsh and died in his home. Beowulf showed Grendel’s arm in Heorot.

Because of her son’s death, Grendel’s mother went to the hall when all warriors were sleeping and killed Aeshere. Beowulf advises they should dive to Grendel’s home and killed Grendel and his mother. Beowulf killed Grendel’s mother with a magical sword, found on the wall of her home. Also, Beowulf found Grendel’s body and brings his head back to Danse. Everyone was celebrating the death of the monsters. Beowulf got lots of treasures from the king of Danse and Greats. A natural, Beowulf became the hero.
